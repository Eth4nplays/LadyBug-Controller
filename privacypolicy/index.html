Privacy Policy for LadyBug Remote

Effective Date: June 5, 2025

RoboForce built the LadyBug Remote app as a free educational tool to control the RoboForce LadyBug learning robot. This service is provided by RoboForce in its current form, without any guarantees or warranties.

1. Information Collection and Use

LadyBug Remote does not collect or store any personal data. The app only requests Nearby Devices permission to enable Bluetooth communication with compatible robots. No user data is collected, transmitted, or stored, either locally or remotely.

2. Permissions

LadyBug Remote requires the Nearby Devices (Bluetooth) permission solely to connect to and control robots like the RoboForce LadyBug. This permission is only used during active sessions and not for any other purpose.

3. External Links

The app may contain buttons or links that direct users to external websites owned and maintained by RoboForce. These websites are operated independently, and once you leave the app, you are subject to the privacy policies of those sites. We recommend reviewing those policies if you have any concerns.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not share any data with third parties. The app does not use any analytics tools, advertising SDKs, or third-party services.

5. Security and Data Retention

Since the app does not collect or store any data, there is no risk of data leakage, misuse, or unauthorized access. There is also no data retained by the app or its developers.

6. User Rights

There are no user accounts, and all operations are performed locally on the user's device. Therefore, there is no stored data to delete, export, or request.

7. Children's Privacy

LadyBug Remote is an educational tool designed to be used by children and students in learning environments. It complies with privacy requirements by not collecting any data whatsoever from users, including children under 13 or under 16 (depending on region).

8. International Availability

This app is available worldwide and complies with general privacy principles by design, given that it handles no personal data.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. If so, changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
